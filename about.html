// bio · background + expertise

Robert Speciale, the founder and CEO of 0xFEED, is an 11-year veteran in the classroom with several years of experience teaching Cybersecurity and Computer Science.

His educational background is backed by a decade-plus career in Information Technology across multiple industries and capacities, bringing real-world tactical knowledge to the next generation of defenders.

A New York City native and lifelong Yankees fan, Mr. Speciale has called Las Vegas home since 2003.

// credentials · academic focus

Mr. Speciale holds a Master's Degree in Educational Technology and a Master's Degree in Educational Psychology & Administration. This dual focus allows him to bridge the gap between complex technical concepts and effective pedagogical delivery.
